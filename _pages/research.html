Research assistant (2017-2021)

Developing non-invasive computational-mechanics and imaging-based diagnostic framework for personalized cardiology for coarctation of aorta disease::

  • Using FVM-based solver (OpenFoam) and LBM-based solver (OpenLB) to model turbulent and transitional flow in large arteries.
  • Using Smagorinsky LES family models to model blood flow.
  • Performing advanced medical imaging codes and techniques to segment and reconstruct CT and MRI Images.
  • Integrate CFD solver with 4D Flow magnetic resonance imaging MATLAB tool.
  • Verifying the computational tool with Taylor-Green vortex and turbulent cavity flow CFD benchmarks.
  • Comparing and validating the computational tool with available personalized 4D flow data, Echocardiography data, Catheter pressure drop data and particle image velocimetry data (PIV)
